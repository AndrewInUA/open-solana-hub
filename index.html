What is Solana?

Solana is public infrastructure for moving, holding, and using digital value – peer to peer, without a bank in the middle. Think of it as a shared ledger anyone can read, with rules enforced by code. That opens a layer of the internet where you can own digital things directly – money, art, tickets, identity – instead of renting access from a company.
